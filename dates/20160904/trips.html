Central Park trip

I finally went to Central Park. Starting at the very top at 110th St and exiting nearly two hours later around the 59th St, it was a calming and wonderful experience. During the walk I completely forgot I’m in a vast and restless city.

It reminded me partially of London parks, especially the enormous size of Regent’s Park, but it was far less orderly and thus more interesting. It looks like a preserved part of a once wild environment, which is strange considering how much planning went into creating it.

As I grew accustomed to in NYC, people were dancing, singing, playing and laughing loudly, there was hardly any quiet space by the time I got to the Alice in Wonderland Statue (I couldn’t get a photo without a horde of children climbing on it). Here you can get lost completely, but never away from people.
