RC - day 17

Achievements:

  • check-in
  • keeping the meditation habit streak
  • warm-up in Python
  • creating a 3D printed paper boat
  • pairing with Rose
  • circle code dojo
  • further work on algorithms
  • reading some “Gödel, Escher, Bach”
  • finally bought a backpack

Most of today went as planned. I got in early, done my daily morning habits of coffee with oatmeal, housekeeping and meditation.

I’ve had a wonderful session of creating a 3D printed paper boat with Jade, writing a wiki entry about it for tomorrow’s Arts & Crafts night.

Pairing with Rose didn’t go as planned, as I don’t know enough about audio analysis and jumped into it too quickly. I’ve learned a lot in terms of next steps and I’m not up for using someone else’s time until I’m ready.

Circle code dojo was fun - we were pair programming in cycle:

  • first person was driving, navigated by the person to their left
  • after 5 minutes we all moved one chair left
  • the first person was then navigating the person to their right

And then everyone would be a driver and a navigator at least once in the cycle. We were working on the good old Mastermind and at some point we’ve managed to win our own game.

Other than that, I was pretty productive with my algorithms and reading. I also bought a backpack, since I have to carry a lot every day and my bag didn’t help with back pains.
