RC - day 18

Achievements:

  • check-in
  • keeping the meditation habit streak
  • warm-up in Python
  • adding RSS feed to the blog
  • watching presentations
  • doing my first live coding
  • creating a dragon, hairpin, magnets and earrings
  • reading some “Gödel, Escher, Bach”
  • taking part in Abstract Salad Factory

Thursday is a bonding day, with the alumni in the space, presentations and events in the evening.

Abstract Salad Factory was just the beginning of it - a group of people brought various ingredients for salads and then we mixed, matched and stuffed ourselves.

My top three community presentations were:

  • Totes Random - an app generating random numbers from tweets by John, Sean and Taylor
  • an expense tracker with some nifty visuals by Leeor and Taylor
  • a presentation on analogies by Naomi

I also took part in Jade’s intro to 3D printing, by doing my first live coding ever. It was stressful, but great, which means I should do it again.

The Arts and Crafts night was full of fun. There was knitting, drawing, sewing, origami, face painting, hairpin glueing, magnet making, eating and laughing. Some Recurses worked in the midst of it all - I admire their ability to focus, wish mine was that good.
