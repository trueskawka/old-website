RC - day 19

Achievements:

  • self check-in
  • keeping the meditation habit streak
  • warm-up in Ruby
  • algorithms (Stanford course) check-in
  • adding a MVP of a calendar to the blog
  • starting a course on CS in Python
  • trying Halo Top ice cream (and eating only half of them)
  • getting a gig as a cat sitter

Fri-yay has come. The day when sleeping in after a busy week is acceptable. The day when going on a long walk just to get protein ice cream is reasonable.

I’ve managed to barely keep my streaks in order today, meditating on the subway while being very late and doing a warm-up right before the algorithms group meeting.

On top of my algorithms course I still have to catch up with the rest of the group, I’ve started a new CS course in Python (because I want to learn the language and ‘proper’ programming better) and I’m getting ready for the course on audio analysis (because I want to implement some of it in my language-learning app). It does seem like much, but structured courses and peer pressure help with motivation, so I’m going to try managing all three of them over the next few weeks.

I wanted to create a simple and fast github-like calendar for my blog, which turned out to be surprisingly difficult, as I’ve managed to forget all of my JS over the past few months. I got to a Minimum Viable Product after a few hours of relearning the language. I’m planning to introduce more web development back into my routine, I don’t want it to get rusty.

As for the cat sitter gig, I got the dibs on spending a weekend with these two sweet fluffs in two weeks, which will be a great opportunity to have a completely distraction free few days (minus the belly rubs), focusing on my learning.
