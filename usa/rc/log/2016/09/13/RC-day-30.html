Achievements:

  • check-in
  • keeping the meditation habit streak
  • having a talk with Nancy about my career goals
  • drafting a presentation on learning

I’ve decided today needs to be a productive day, despite the stress with the Airbnb. Refocusing on growing helped a lot, as did a cold shower from a loved one.

I had a chat with Nancy about my career goals and decided on the next steps I’m going to take in order to improve my professional presence:

  • refactoring some of my older projects that I can quickly improve and use in my portfolio
  • writing long overdue blog posts for my official blog
  • working more on the GitBook, as it is a project with community values in mind
  • looking into remote work opportunities

I’ve also reread a book on acquiring new skills - First 20 Hours by Josh Kaufman - and I’m going to use the concepts to improve my learning over the next few weeks. I also want to have a short presentation on the topic, since it is a system of simple concepts that can be useful to others.

In terms of long-term goals, I’ve revised my bucket, project, learning and productive procrastination lists (I seem to love lists for some reason). I’ve changed some priorities and aligned them more with my programming skills.
