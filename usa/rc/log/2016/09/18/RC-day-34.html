RC - day 34

Achievements:

  • watching Objectified
  • awesome women’s night at RC

Cats, books and Stranger Things kept me entertained most of the day. The kitties are playful and curious, constantly nagging me for attention. Focusing was not an easy task.

We’ve also had a great women’s night at RC, thanks to Isla & Kaylyn (and RC staff, of course), discussing some of our experiences and developing strategies and actionable solutions to common problems.

I especially loved two simple strategies presented by Alicia:

  • inappropriate comment

    • ask the person to repeat their comment, to avoid ambiguity (e.g. what did you say? what do you mean by that? excuse me?)
    • label the comment/language (e.g. that’s ignorant/sexist/dismissive), not the person
    • get back to work (after all, you are paid to work and hopefully so is the other person)

  • unwelcome behaviour

    Instead of reacting on the spot, it’s best to talk in person, e.g. “I feel like you interrupt me, have you noticed?” - that’s a simple yes or no question and both answers are great!

    1. yes - “good! please don’t do that again”
    2. no - “no problem, how about I just let you know if I notice it again?” (opening for further improvement)

It is much easier to react and solve problems when we can name them. And we don’t feel crazy, because it’s not “just you” or “an incident” - it’s something many women experience daily.

We also came up with an idea for an app to register unwanted behaviours and easily visualise them, which I hope to mob code with my awesome RC friends over the next week.

I’m rarely in all women environments and it was freeing to experience that. Without men around we behave differently, focusing on communication rather than perceptions.
