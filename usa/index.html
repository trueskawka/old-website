first steps

I'm still not sure what time it is, I'm floating somewhere between noon and 2pm.

Eva and Shawn are great. They've shown me around and are making sure I'm comfortable and taken care of. They're like a family abroad.

Upper Manhattan reminds me of London - brick buildings, loads of local shops and loads of parks. It's also really dense and varied. Walking two blocks down takes you to a very different area.

The heat melts away any thoughts of walking around before evening, so I guess it'll be a quiet first day in and a late walk to find the RC.
