08/21/2016 14:35

first sightseeing trip

Went sightseeing with a group of RC friends. We decided to start early in the morning, as most tourists would probably sleep in.

The One World Observatory was both amazing and tacky. The elevator ride was scary but loads of fun and took about 47 seconds to go up 102 floors. The views were amazing - just seeing the city and knowing how tall the tiny buildings below are. But the commercial side was a bit surprising and very tacky. The sales pitches, the useless big screens with loads of videos, the gift shop full of awful knick-knocks - everything to convince us it was worth spending more than $30 for the ride up.

The WTC memorial is amazing. Quiet, simple and peaceful. Two fountains flowing slowly and steadily into nothingness.

Sadly, when we got to Wall Street, there were so many tourists grabbing the bull statue by the balls, I didn't get to take a picture this time.
